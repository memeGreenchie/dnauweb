What is Datanaut?

Datanaut is a decentralized AI trading assistant platform where users contribute insights, validate information, and access real-time AI-driven market analysis.

What makes Datanaut unique?

Unlike traditional tools, Datanaut leverages community-driven validation and AI orchestration to provide smarter, transparent trading support.

Which blockchain is Datanaut built on?

Datanaut runs on Binance Smart Chain (BSC), chosen for its low fees, high throughput, and strong DeFi infrastructure.

Who can join the Datanaut ecosystem?

Traders, AI enthusiasts, developers, and token holders can participate by using the AI bot, validating data, or engaging in governance.

What is $DNAU?

$DNAU is the utility token of the Datanaut ecosystem, used for rewards, staking, access to tools, and governance participation.

How is $DNAU allocated?

$DNAU is distributed among the community, platform growth, staking rewards, development, marketing, and a reserve fund.

Can I stake $DNAU?

Yes, staking $DNAU grants you access to premium features and earns rewards based on your participation and duration.

How do I earn $DNAU?

You can earn $DNAU by using the AI bot, validating data, staking, or being among top contributors within the ecosystem.

What services does Datanaut offer?

Datanaut provides AI-powered trading insights, a Telegram mini-app assistant, validation tasks, and contributor-based earning features.

How does the Telegram mini-app work?

Users interact with the AI assistant inside Telegram to ask market questions, validate AI responses, and earn $DNAU for quality feedback.

Is the AI truly decentralized?

Yes. All AI results are refined through community voting and validator consensus to maintain decentralization and fairness.

Do I need $DNAU to use the platform?

Basic use is free, but $DNAU unlocks advanced features, high-frequency access, and participation in DAO governance.

When is the beta launching?

The public beta of the Datanaut mini-app is already live with 80,000+ users. Continuous updates are being rolled out toward the full launch in August 2025.

What are the key milestones in the roadmap?

Major milestones include beta deployment, AI model optimization, full launch in August 2025, and global expansion in early 2026.

What happens after launch?

Post-launch, the focus will shift to community governance, new AI feature sets, and strategic integrations with crypto platforms.

Where can I track progress?

You can track updates via our roadmap page, official announcements, and our community channels including Telegram and X.
